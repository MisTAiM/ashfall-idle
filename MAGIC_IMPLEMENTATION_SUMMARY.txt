PROGRESSION TIMELINE
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

0-15 MINUTES (First Spell)
  ├─ Kill 10-20 rats
  ├─ Collect 5 essence_of_mind
  ├─ Craft Mind Rune (2 sec cast)
  ├─ Have mana 8+
  └─ Cast Spark! First spell unlocked

15-30 MINUTES (Experimenting)
  ├─ Kill goblins → collect air essence
  ├─ Craft air runes
  ├─ Discover Ice Bolt at level 25 magic
  ├─ Heal spell available at level 10
  └─ Have 3-5 spells in rotation

30-60 MINUTES (Scaling)
  ├─ Kill skeletons → water essence
  ├─ Kill wolves → fire essence
  ├─ Unlock Fireball (3-rune requirement)
  ├─ Have 6-8 spells available
  └─ Magic XP flowing from all sources

60+ MINUTES (Specialization)
  ├─ Farm high-level monsters
  ├─ Access rare essences (chaos, ancient)
  ├─ Unlock endgame spells
  ├─ Build rune stockpiles
  └─ Pure choice in playstyle

VERDICT: Smooth progression, no grinds, constant feeling of growth
