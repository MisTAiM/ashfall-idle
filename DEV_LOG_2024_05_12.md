[PHASE 4] SPELL BALANCE TUNING (10 minutes)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Task: Adjust spell costs and damage for proper balance
Status: ✓ COMPLETE

Changes Made:

SPARK (Entry Level)
  Before: 10 mana, 1-4 damage, 1 fire rune required
  After:  8 mana, 3-8 damage, 1 mind rune required
  Why: Fire rune is wrong element; mind is logical progression
       Lower mana cost (8 is easier to access)
       Higher damage (3-8 avg 5.5 = viable for farming)

FIREBALL (Power Spike)
  Before: 20 mana, 2-8 damage, air + fire x2 runes
  After:  16 mana, 5-15 damage, mind + fire x2 runes
  Why: Higher damage (justified by level 20 requirement)
       Fixed rune cost (mind makes sense as base)
       Level 20 feels like true power increase

ICE BOLT (Utility)
  Before: 18 mana, 2-6 damage, 2 water runes
  After:  15 mana, 4-12 damage, 1 mind + 2 water runes
  Why: Higher damage range (utility should still hit hard)
       Stun effect remains (primary feature)
       Mind rune anchor (consistency)

METEOR STRIKE (Endgame)
  Before: 40 mana, 4-12 damage, mind x2 + fire x2 + chaos x1
  After:  35 mana, 8-20 damage, mind + fire x2 + chaos x1
  Why: Damage doubled (8-20 = true ultimate spell)
       Mana reduced slightly (still high cost at 35)
       Fewer mind runes (more accessible)
       Cost justifies power (4 rune requirement gates it)

HEALING SPELLS (Balance)
  Heal: 12 mana, 1-20 HP healing (avg 10.5)
        = 0.88 hp per mana (efficient utility)
  Why: Healing should be viable option
       Not better than damage (0.88 < 0.69 dps/mana damage)
       But covers all roles (mage can solo)

DPS Analysis (Damage Per Mana):
  Spark:         5.5 / 8 = 0.69 dps/mana
  Fireball:     10 / 16 = 0.63 dps/mana
  Ice Bolt:     8 / 15 = 0.53 dps/mana (+ stun bonus)
  Meteor Strike: 14 / 35 = 0.40 dps/mana (endgame = less efficient)

Verdict: Consistent scaling, all spells viable
