Core Loop

Train skills to gather resources → craft gear → fight stronger monsters → get better drops → train higher skills. Quests unlock special items and large XP rewards. Dungeons give the best drops. Guilds unlock shared banking and bonuses.

Saving Your Progress

The game auto-saves locally every 30 seconds. Create an account (Account page) to enable cloud saves that sync across all your devices. Cloud saves are automatic every 60 seconds when signed in.

Combat

Ashfall Idle uses a tick-based combat system. You attack once per tick automatically. Combat level determines which areas and monsters you can access.

Combat Level Formula

Combat Level = 0.25 × (Defence + Hitpoints + Prayer/2) + 0.325 × max(Attack+Strength, Ranged×2, Magic×2)

Prayers

21 prayers split into Melee, Ranged, Magic, and Protection categories. Active prayers drain Prayer Points. Refill by burying bones — higher-tier bones restore more points. Dragon bones are the most efficient.

Abilities & Special Attacks

Unlock combat abilities as you level. Special attacks charge a bar and deal massive burst damage. Use the potion belt (4 slots) for quick access to boosts during combat.

Loadout Swap

Save up to 4 named gear loadouts in the combat screen. Swap between them instantly mid-fight. Your old gear returns to your bank automatically.

Food & Auto-Eat

Stock the Food Bag before combat. Enable Auto-Eat to automatically eat when HP drops below 40%. Higher-tier food heals more HP.

Game Mechanics

Offline Progression

The game calculates offline gains when you return. Skills continue training, gathering resources and XP. Combat does not run offline. The offline cap is 24 hours.

Alignment System

Two axes: Moral (Good ↔ Evil) and Order (Lawful ↔ Chaotic). Your alignment shifts based on your actions:

ActionMoral ShiftOrder Shift
Kill evil monster+1 Good—
Kill innocent creature+2 Evil+1 Chaotic
Kill neutral creature—+1 Chaotic
Quest: moral choice±3–25±3–15

Reputation

Each faction (Silver Order, Ashen Guild, Bloodfang Clan, Veiled Circle) has its own reputation track. Higher rep unlocks exclusive shop items and quest access.

Slayer System

A Slayer Master assigns tasks to kill a specific number of a certain monster. Completing tasks grants Slayer XP. While on task: +15% drop rate, eligible for unique slayer drops.

Mastery

Each action within a skill has a Mastery level (0–99). Higher mastery increases efficiency: faster actions, better resource yields, or higher XP per action.

Pets

18 pets available. Each gives a passive bonus when active (e.g. +5% woodcutting speed, +1% drop rate). Found as rare drops from skills or combat.

Online Features

Ashfall Idle uses Firebase for real-time multiplayer. All social features require creating an account (free).

Accounts

Anonymous players can chat and play locally but cannot use cloud saves, friend lists, or guilds. Create a free account via email or Google Sign-In to unlock all online features. Progress is saved to the cloud every 60 seconds.

Global Chat

Real-time chat visible to all online players. Messages show player name, level tag, and timestamp. System messages (server announcements) appear in amber.

Guilds

Create a guild for 1,000 gold. Guilds have 7 ranks: Recruit → Member → Sergeant → Lieutenant → Captain → General → Leader. The Leader can configure rank permissions, deposit/withdraw item bank rules, emblem, and description. The gold bank and item bank are shared between all members based on rank permissions.

Friends System

Send friend requests by searching for any player's name. Incoming requests appear in the Friends page. Accepted friends can send private messages. Both sent and received requests show as pending until accepted.

PvP Arena

Challenge other players to 1v1 combat. Your gear and stats are used in the fight simulation. Winners gain rating; losers lose rating. Bounties can be placed on specific players.

Bazaar (Player Market)

List items for sale at your set price. Other players can buy instantly. Gold from sales is held until you collect it from the Bazaar page.
